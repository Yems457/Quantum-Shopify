Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2026

Welcome to Quantum-Shopify. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data Usage

This privacy policy informs you how we look after your personal data when you visit our website Quantum-Shopify.ca and explains your privacy rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase electronics or interact with our store, we collect the following types of information:

  1. Details: Name, billing address, shipping address, email address, and phone number.
  2. Payment Information: We securely collect transaction and order data. Note that full credit card details are processed and tokenized by our payment gateway providers and are not stored on our servers.
  3. Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, products, and services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  1. Order Processing: To process and deliver your orders, manage returns, and provide customer support.
  2. Communication: To send order confirmations, tracking details, or promotional electronic newsletters (with your consent).
  3. Security & Fraud Prevention: To protect our website and operations against unauthorized access and fraud.

3. Sharing and Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share your data only with trusted third parties:

  1. Payment Processors: Such as Stripe or PayPal, which adhere to PCI-DSS standards.
  2. Shipping Providers: Such as DHL or local postal services, for the delivery of your electronic goods.
  3. Analytics Providers: Such as Google Analytics, to improve website performance.

4. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. While we use SSL encryption, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Legal Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  1. Access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data.
  2. Object to processing for direct marketing purposes.
